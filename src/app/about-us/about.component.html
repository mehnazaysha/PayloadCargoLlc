About Payload

Payload Cargo LLC is one of the leading privately owned logistics company in the UAE providing cost effective logistics solutions worldwide.We believe in reliable , high quality , value for money freight solutions to our customers.

At Payload Cargo we are committed in delivering the best freight and logistics solutions globally. We develop comprehensive tailor made project logistics to meet our customer needs.Our team is air and sea freight experts in delivering, customers can rely on Payload Cargo for Fixed/Blocked space capacities with all major carriers worldwide.

Our knowledge coupled with international management puts PAYLOAD CARGO in the forefront in offering the best logistics solutions to our clients with complete supply chain management from door to door. We provide a combination of cost-effective logistics services to our clients worldwide.

We will at all time tailor our products to precisely meet the needs of our clients.

We deliver superior quality service and good value for our clients. We offer value added service that include pre-shipment services like pick and pack , special packaging services and 24hours customer service. We are located at the Air Cargo city, Freight gate-5 at Dubai Airport Free Zone.(DAFZ).
